About Us

In February 2025, a passionate university English teacher founded The English Lab to create a welcoming space where students' work could shine. This initiative, rooted in the Science Department of the University of Le Mans, is all about celebrating the projects students undertake as part of their English course. These projects span various fields, from acoustics and STEPS (Sport and Exercise Science) to actuarial science and IT, with more to come. Feel free to explore the website and see what they've been up to!
